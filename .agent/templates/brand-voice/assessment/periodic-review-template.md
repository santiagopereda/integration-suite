## Section 2: Drift Detection

- Where has voice drifted from the profile? (Specific examples)
- Is the drift intentional evolution or unintentional inconsistency?
- Which voice attributes are most consistently applied?
- Which attributes are most frequently violated?
- Are there new content creators who haven't adopted the voice?

## Section 3: Market Context

- Has the competitive landscape changed?
- Has audience expectation shifted?
- Are there industry trends affecting communication norms?
- Has the brand's positioning or strategy changed?
- Are there new channels requiring voice adaptation?

## Section 4: Stakeholder Feedback

- Content team: What's working? What's hard to maintain?
- Audience: Sentiment analysis, engagement metrics, direct feedback
- Leadership: Does the voice still align with business direction?
